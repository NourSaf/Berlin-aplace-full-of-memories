ABOUT

This interactive map is a personal narrative, charting my experiences in Berlin from 2016 to 2024. Each marker reveals a story of places I've lived, worked, and explored, blending photographs and music to bring these memories to life serving as a documentation for my future self and friends 30 years from now.

Berlin has been a canvas for my growth, adventures, and reflections. This website aims to share that journey, showcasing how the city's diverse locales have contributed to my story.

Berlin, a place full of memories, a project created during Nina Bender's "Mapping Stories" course. This project is a celebration of how places shape our narratives, hoping to inspire you to map your own journey and the significant places within it.
